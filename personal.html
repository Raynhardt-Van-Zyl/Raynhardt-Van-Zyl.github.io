About Me

As a dedicated researcher and innovator, I hold a master's degree in Robotics Engineering and bring a multidisciplinary background spanning Robotics, Neuroscience, Farming, Power, and Finance. I have honed a diverse skill set, including heterogeneous computing, robotic arm design, and algorithmic trading systems. Driven by curiosity and a commitment to excellence, I have a proven track record in delivering innovative solutions and conducting high-quality research. My career goal is to leverage my broad scientific knowledge and unique interdisciplinary perspective to contribute to groundbreaking research and make a significant impact in my chosen field of study.

Interests

Science

My interest in science stems from its ability to explain the world around us. From a young age, I was fascinated by the inner workings of things, often disassembling broken appliances to understand their assembly. This curiosity has grown into a deep appreciation for the explanatory power of science.

Nature

The complexity and beauty of nature have always captivated me. Growing up in a nature reserve, I was immersed in the natural world from an early age. This exposure fostered a deep appreciation for the intricacies of our environment, from the growth of my first vegetable garden to the wildlife that shared our space.

Religion

Religion, particularly Christianity, has been a significant part of my life. Raised in a devout Christian family, I've been guided by the teachings of Jesus Christ and the existential questions posed by religion. I continue to explore what it means to be a Christian in today's society, particularly in relation to questions of life and death.

Hobbies

My hobbies are a reflection of my interests and values. Squash, which I played at a university level, provides a physical challenge and a way to manage stress. Cycling, an activity I've enjoyed since my school years, offers a way to relax and enjoy the outdoors. 3D printing taps into my love for creation and innovation, while outdoor rock climbing allows me to engage directly with the natural world, reaching places few others have been.

Personal Philosophy

At the core of my philosophy is the desire to make a positive impact on the world, guided by the teachings of Jesus. I strive to live a life that, at its end, reflects a commitment to improving the world around me, regardless of awards or financial success.

Inspirations

My family - my mother Rosemary, my father Vincent, and my sister Vide - have been a constant source of love and inspiration. Above all, the teachings and disciples of Jesus Christ inspire me to live a life of compassion, love, and service.
